VicesFest 2025

Come join us for an amazing weekend this summer at The Strand Theater. Featuring bands from all over the USA and beyond.

If you want to participate or perform at this years VicesFest, please send your info to submissions@vicesfest.com

Cut off date to submit your material is May 2nd. All submissions will be reviewed by our staff and you will be contacted if you are picked.

If you or your company are looking to be part of the sponsorship board, please email j.hicks@vicesfest.com for more details.

The festival will be filmed for a documentary that will be featured on Prime and on other platforms.

Many more details will be coming soon, so please keep looking for updates.
