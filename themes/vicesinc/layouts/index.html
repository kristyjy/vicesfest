VicesFest 2025

Welcome to VicesFest 2025! Join us at The Strand Dover for three days filled with fun, music, and indulgence. Come and see your new favorite musicians in a safe lively atmosphere. From live music, vendors, delicious food and drinks to exciting activities, VicesFest has something for everyone. Get ready to let loose and enjoy a weekend of pure entertainment. Don't miss out on this unforgettable experience - mark your calendars now!
